MY COMMUTE
London has 1.4 million daily commuters making the same blind gamble every morning — leaving home without knowing if the tube is running. My Commute fixes that silently. Three layers work in perfect coordination so you never have to open the app: a Push Notification surfaces disruptions the moment they're detected — a home-screen Widget shows your line's live status at a glance — and a Dynamic Island alert pulses your commute signal as you walk out the door. All three fire seamlessly together. Zero opens. Total awareness. V1 architecture complete. Ready to scale.

Chapter 01 · The Origin
A broken Tube.
A brother.
A decision.
College → part-time job → running marketing for businesses — learning what makes people stop and pay attention. Then one evening, watching my brother walk through the door exhausted after another unannounced Tube failure, I decided to build the app London was missing. No CS degree. No coding background. Not a single line of code written in my life before this. Just obsession, AI as a co-pilot, and a problem worth solving.
AI as co-pilot. Zero prior code. Working app. [+]
Chapter 02 · The Build
AI as co-pilot.
Zero prior code.
Working app.
Built entirely with AI — React Native, the TfL API, Dynamic Island integration across 14 lines and 270+ stations. A non-technical founder shipped a working app because the right tools exist now and the right problem was clear. Every interaction was directed, not assembled. Every silence is deliberate. The app never asks for your attention — it earns it before you even open it.

Funded by freelance SaaS work. Every client project teaches something that goes straight back into My Commute. The hustle is the curriculum.
